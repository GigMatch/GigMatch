About GigMatch

GigMatch is a virtual platform for connections between musicians and venues in real time across multiple cities. Musicians can connect with other musicians to help fill a spot for their next gig or find that special venue in need of musical talent. Allows a streamlined approach to seeking talent and a space for music to be shared and discussed.
